Gym

Gym sessions have become an integral part of my daily routine. The exhilarating rush of endorphins that accompanies a good workout is unmatched. Not only does it keep me physically fit, but it also serves as a great stress buster, allowing me to refocus and re-energize for the day ahead.

Bicycling

To school

Opting for a bicycle as my mode of transportation to school has been both environmentally friendly and health-conscious. It's a refreshing way to kick-start my day, allowing me to enjoy the fresh morning breeze and scenic views along the way, all while reducing my carbon footprint.

For fun

Bicycling for leisure is an exhilarating experience. Exploring picturesque trails and cycling routes has become one of my favorite pastimes. It not only keeps me active but also provides an opportunity to appreciate the beauty of nature and embrace a sense of freedom and adventure.

Hanging out with the boys

Spending quality time with my close-knit group of friends is something I cherish deeply. Whether it's engaging in spirited discussions, playing competitive sports, or simply enjoying each other's company, these moments contribute significantly to my sense of belonging and well-being.

Web development

Web development has been a passion of mine since an early age. The ability to create visually appealing and functional websites is a source of endless excitement and satisfaction. From coding to designing, every aspect of the process fuels my creativity and allows me to stay updated with the latest technological advancements, fostering a continuous learning journey.

During this past summer, I teamed up with two of my friends to work on an exciting project. We've been developing an engaging online platform for a unique drinking game that infuses traditional party activities with a digital twist. Our aim is to bring people together virtually, creating unforgettable and hilarious moments, even from a distance. To learn more about this project and my other experiences in web development, you can visit my experience tab.
